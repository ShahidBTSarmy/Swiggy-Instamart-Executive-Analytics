Executive Business Analytics Dashboard for Swiggy Instamart
Project Overview

This project is a Business Intelligence and Executive Decision Support System developed using Power BI, DAX, and Excel.

The dashboard analyzes sales performance, customer behavior, operational efficiency, and future demand trends for a quick-commerce business model similar to Swiggy Instamart.

The objective is to transform raw business data into actionable insights that support strategic decision-making.

Business Problem

Quick-commerce companies operate in highly competitive environments where success depends on:

Fast deliveries
High customer satisfaction
Efficient warehouse operations
Revenue growth
Data-driven decision making

This project helps management monitor key business metrics and identify opportunities for improvement.

Project Objectives
Analyze overall sales performance
Evaluate customer behavior and satisfaction
Measure delivery efficiency
Monitor warehouse performance
Identify operational bottlenecks
Forecast future revenue trends
Provide strategic business recommendations

Dashboard Structure
Dashboard 1 – Sales Performance Dashboard

Features:

Total Sales Analysis
Average Sales Tracking
Product Category Performance
Area-wise Sales Analysis
City-wise Revenue Analysis
Gender-based Analysis

Key Findings:

Bangalore generated the highest revenue.
Fruits & Vegetables emerged as the top-performing category.
Revenue contribution remained balanced across genders.
Dashboard 2 – Executive Overview Dashboard

Features:

Revenue Forecasting
Customer Sentiment Analysis
Rating Analysis
Business Insights Summary

Key Findings:

Positive customer sentiment exceeded 75%.
Revenue forecasts indicate stable future demand.
Customer ratings strongly influence revenue generation.
Dashboard 3 – Operations Performance Dashboard

Features:

Delivery Status Monitoring
Delivery Efficiency Analysis
Warehouse Performance Evaluation
Traffic Impact Analysis
Payment Method Analysis

Key Findings:

Average delivery time: 24.73 minutes
On-time delivery rate: 77.42%
High traffic conditions increase delivery delays
Dashboard 4 – Strategic Insights Dashboard

Features:

Executive KPIs
Revenue vs Delivery Performance
Operational Insights
Strategic Recommendations

Key Findings:

Bangalore is the primary revenue driver.
Delivery delays increase during peak traffic periods.
Digital payments dominate customer transactions.

Key Business Insights

Revenue Performance
Total Revenue: $1.50M
Bangalore contributed the highest revenue share.
Revenue concentration exists in major urban markets.
Customer Experience
Average Customer Rating: 4.0/5
Positive sentiment exceeded 75%.
Customer satisfaction remains consistently strong.

Operational Performance
Average Delivery Time: 24.73 minutes
On-Time Delivery Rate: 77.42%
Warehouse performance remained relatively balanced.

Predictive Insights
Revenue forecasts suggest stable future demand.
Current operational model appears sustainable.

Strategic Recommendations

1. Expand Bangalore Operations

Increase inventory allocation and delivery capacity in high-performing regions.

2. Improve Delivery Efficiency

Deploy additional delivery partners during peak traffic periods.

3. Strengthen Customer Loyalty

Introduce personalized offers and rewards programs.

4. Optimize Warehouse Operations

Implement standardized performance scorecards across warehouses.
